Maureen Johnston

I have always loved to code, starting very early in my career writing assembler, and writing in a number of programming languages for different applications. I put my coding on pause while raising my children and dove back in over the last six years. While working to gain an expert level in Excel and VBA I realized I needed a better understanding of object-oriented level programming so I immersed myself in the Oracle Java SE 7 Programmer I and II development courses.

Since then I've become very excited about Ruby on Rails web development. After attending The Flatiron School I have been inspired by this thriving community.

I am a problem-solver at heart, have a passion for learning, building new products, testing and writing quality code.
